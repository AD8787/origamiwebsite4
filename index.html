Interesting facts about chameleon.

  • Chameleons can change their skin color to blend in the environment, or camelflage.
  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues.

Interesting Facts about pandas.

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • They spend a lot of their day eating bamboo.
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.

Interesting facts about Pigeons.

  • In the UK, Pigeons were used to track air pollution to help spread awareness on climate change.
  • They primarily feed on seeds, fruits, and plants.
  • These are stout-bodied birds with short necks, and short slender bills that in some species feature fleshy ceres.
